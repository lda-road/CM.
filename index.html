About Joji & The Album

Joji, also known as George Miller, is a Japanese singer-songwriter whose introspective and emotional sound has redefined lo-fi and R&B. SMITHEREENS (2022) is his third studio album, capturing heartbreak, vulnerability, and growth in a minimal yet cinematic way.

The album blends stripped-back piano ballads like “Glimpse of Us” with dreamy textures such as “YUKON (Interlude)”, showcasing Joji’s delicate balance of raw emotion and subtle production.
